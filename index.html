About Me

My primary learning interests are in computer systems, data analysis and modeling, and high-performance computing.

Academic Lineage

I am a graduate and 'double husky' of Northeastern University, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from September 2017 to May 2021. My coursework included Investments, Fixed-Income Securities, Financial Data Analysis, and Corporate Finance. From January 2022 to May 2024, I earned a Master of Science in Computer Science. Coursework included Algorithms, Computer Systems, Software Vulnerabilities, and a few Computer Graphics courses taught by Professor Mike Shah. My coursework taught me a plethora of programming languages and paradigms and amassed skills such as non-interactive raytracing, reverse engineering, full stack development with RESTful APIs, and data analytics among others. During my academic career, I was involved with various on-campus organizations, including the Mascot team, NU Symphony Orchestra, and a fraternity where I served as Secretary.

Professional Work Experience

As an Audit Analyst at Fidelity Investments, I have honed my expertise in both operational and IT general controls, participating in various audits over the firm's asset management, operations, and digital assets groups. I have performed audits on Equity Trading, ETF Operations, Derivatives, compliance, soft dollar, and digital assets custody teams. My prior experience as a Data Reconciliation Specialist at Financial Recovery Technologies enabled me to master SQL scripting for efficient data handling and perform detailed analyses on trade data, enhancing my ability to identify and resolve data discrepancies. While in school, I developed leadership and marketing skills as the school mascot, successfully completed two co-ops at Eaton Vance and Wayfair, and served in various part-time marketing roles for the Boston Bruins and Boston Red Sox.

Projects

React Web App

This is Driver, a motorsports-themed social media platform. A web app built with React, Node.js, and MongoDB to create a Formula 1-focused social media platform. Makes extensive use of the Ergast API to keep the app up-to-date on the latest race, Driver and Constructor standings, and historic data on every Formula 1 driver. Note, the platform is not active so new users/speeds will not load.

Raytracer Optimization Using Bounding Volume Hierarchy

This Raytracer was built in my Non-interactive Computer Graphics course. Written in C++ and custom math libraries, this project involved implementing a BVH structure to optimize rendering speed for a raytracer built during Noninteractive Computer Graphics.

Procedural Generation of 3D Buildings

Using OpenGL, this project places the user on Mars in Space with a skybox and (poor) lighting techniques. The magic of the project lies in the algorithm used to randomly generate structures on the planet.

2D Game Engine

Developed using SDL 3, the Fortune Engine is a (barebones) framework for creating 2D games, containing classes for players, scenes, and texturing capabilities.

Music

I have played the violin for over 15 years having performed in various orchestras and ensembles. I played in Repertory and Principal divisions at Florida Youth Orchestra from 2013-2015. My family and I then moved to Texas where I did high school orchestra until graduation in 2017. Right after my freshman year of college, I had the pleasure to play at Harvard Summer School Orchestra with my best friend Andre, where we performed a duet together. It was quite a change from high school orchestra, and I will always cherish that summer! Today, I enjoy tutoring students wishing to learn the violin.

Martial Arts

As a 2nd-degree black belt in Songahm Taekwondo under the American Taekwondo Association, I have competed in over fifty tournaments and have taught classes to students of all ages. My greatest achievement (so far!) has been earning the title of Florida State Champion in three categories: sparring, weapons, and forms. My favorite part of the sport was watching my peers and myself grow as martial artists, athletes, and human beings. Hockey

Gaining some practice ice skating from my mascot duties, I signed up for a local adult hockey league for a few games of pickup a year and sometimes with my friends when the ponds freeze in the winter.
